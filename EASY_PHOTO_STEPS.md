## Quick Steps to Add Photos

### Adding Gallery Photos (Most Common):

1. **Save your photos** with names like:
   - `image16.jpg` (if you have 15 photos already)
   - `image17.jpg`, `image18.jpg`, etc.

2. **Put them in the right folder**:
   - Open the `images/gallery/` folder
   - Copy your photos there

3. **Send them to GitHub**:
   - Open GitHub Desktop
   - You'll see your new photos listed
   - Write a short message like "Add new birthday photos"
   - Click "Commit to master"
   - Click "Push origin"

4. **Wait 1 minute** and refresh your website!

### Updating Your Profile Photo:

1. **Save your new photo** as exactly:
   - `profile.jpg`

2. **Replace the old one**:
   - Open the `images/profile/` folder
   - Replace `profile.jpg` with your new photo

3. **Send to GitHub** (same as above):
   - GitHub Desktop will show the change
   - Write "Update profile photo"
   - Click "Commit to master"
   - Click "Push origin"

4. **Wait 1 minute** and refresh your website!

## Need Help?

If you get stuck, just remember:
- **Gallery photos**: Name them `image16.jpg`, `image17.jpg`, etc. and put in `images/gallery/`
- **Profile photo**: Name it `profile.jpg` and put in `images/profile/`
- **Send to GitHub**: Use GitHub Desktop to commit and push
- **Wait and refresh**: Changes appear in about 1 minute

## Photo Tips

- JPG format works best
- Keep photos under 2MB each (most phone photos are fine)
- Square photos (same width and height) look best for profile
- Landscape photos (wide) work well in the gallery
